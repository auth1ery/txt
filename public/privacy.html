Last updated: January 11, 2026

What we collect:

We collect your nicknames, your posts and comments, your timestamps (when you made the post/when you commented), and your votes (upvote/downvote).

We use localStorage to store your client side data and all server side actions are on Render's servers. See Render's Privacy Policy for how they handle server side actions.

We collect IP addresses for bans. Moderators, and the owner (auth) cannot and never will view IP addresses. We do not sell your IP addresses.

By clicking "delete account" you fully agree that 1. all of your posts and comments and votes will be deleted and 2. your profile, IP address, and any data tied to you will be deleted.

We do not use cookies, and never will.
